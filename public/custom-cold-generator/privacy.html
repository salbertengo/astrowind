Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2025

1. Data we access

  • Google Sheets ranges you explicitly select in the sidebar.
  • Your Google-account e-mail (to track usage quota and licence status).
  • Optional OpenAI API key you paste in the add-on. The key is stored only in your personal UserProperties; no one at Pampa & Código can view or export it.

2. How we use data

  • Create the AI email content you ask for (the selected cell values and the scraped webpage are sent to OpenAI’s API).
  • Increment a usage counter to enforce the free-trial limit.

3. Storage & retention

  • No sheet content or scraped HTML is persisted on any server; processing happens in-memory during the request.
  • Your API key is encrypted by Google Apps Script and never leaves your Google account.
  • Usage counters are deleted automatically 7 days after you uninstall the add-on.

4. Sharing

  • We never sell or share data with third parties other than:
  • • OpenAI LLC – receives only the prompt text you instruct us to send, for the sole purpose of generating the email.
  • • Google APIs – limited to add-on functionality and compliant with the Google API Services User Data Policy.

5. Your controls

  • You may delete generated emails in your sheet; we keep no copies.
